The Opportunity

Legacy systems.
Petabytes of data.
Almost zero intelligence.

Airlines generate more operational and commercial data than almost any other industry on Earth. Most of it sits in 1990s-era systems or disconnected silos. The result: lost revenue, expensive disruptions, and passengers treated like transactions instead of individuals.

THE PROBLEM

Fragmented data + legacy thinking

Yield, crew, maintenance, passenger interactions, and ancillary all live in separate worlds. Decisions are still made on intuition or yesterday's reports.

THE MARKET

Regional carriers & LCCs need it most

They compete on experience and ancillary margin but lack the headcount and IT budgets of legacy giants. They need intelligence that scales without armies of analysts.

THE PRIZE

$15B+ serviceable opportunity

The airline intelligence layer is a wide-open category. No one else combines deep airline domain expertise with production-grade, real-time AI infrastructure.

Why AeroZag Wins

Airline-deep and AI-native.
That combination is almost nonexistent.

Generic IT services firms treat airlines as one of twenty verticals. Aviation consultancies advise brilliantly but cannot build or run systems. AeroZag is the only partner that understands yield, slots, crew legality, and MRO at an expert level — and can build, deploy, and operate the AI that optimizes them continuously.
